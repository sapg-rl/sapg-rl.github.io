PPO with different exploration strategies

We test different methods for exploration such as RND and DIAYN-based rewards and observed that these methods obtain similar subpar performance as in PPO. To use DIAYN in our setting, we use it as an additive intrinsic reward trying to generate diversity in the policies, although we could not find any prior work that does so. To the best of our knowledge, DIAYN-based rewards are generally used to learn different skills and not maximize reward in a particular environment.


Measuring diversity

We also quantitatively analyze the diversity of states visited by our method during training using two different methods. The first involves using PCA and plotting reconstruction error against number of PCA components used.
The second method involves training an smaller MLP to reconstruct inputs and evaluating training error on batches of states from different methods.
